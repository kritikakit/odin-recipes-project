Lasagna

DESCRIPTION

Lasagna is made of Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly. Lasagna originated in Italy during the Middle Ages.

INGREDIENTS

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • vegetables

STEPS TO MAKE LASAGNA

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the ground beef in a pan.
  3. Layer the lasagna noodles, ricotta, mozzarella cheese and meat sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Bake until it turns into a nice golden colour.
  5. Slioce and serve hot
