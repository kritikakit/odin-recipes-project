Pasta

DESCRIPTION

Italian pasta (pronounced “PAS-tah”) is a collective name for a category of food made from wheat flour and water, sometimes with egg. The name refers to the resulting dough (pasta also means “dough”) that is rolled out and cut into various shapes. Pasta is cooked in water and served with a sauce.

INGREDIENTS

  • Any Pasta
  • heavy cream
  • Dry mustard
  • cheddar cheese
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, grated
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell peppers
  • Italian sausage
  • Parsley
  • Olives
  • onions

STEPS TO MAKE PASTA

  1. Put the pasta into a pan to boil
  2. take another pan and add vegetables to it
  3. After vegetables turn to brown add mustard, cream to it
  4. Add sharp cheddar. Parmigiano and Parsley followed by stirring
  5. Add pasta to it
  6. Mix it thoroughly
  7. Serve immediately
