Pizza

DESCRIPTION

Pizza is an oven-baked flat bread typically topped with a tomato sauce, cheese and various toppings. The modern pizza was invented in Naples, Italy, and the dish has since become popular in many parts of the world.

INGREDIENTS

  • Dry yeast and flour
  • olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • mozarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Bell peppers
  • Italian sausage
  • Fresh Basil
  • Olives
  • Pepperoni,onions,Ham

STEPS TO MAKE PIZZA

  1. MAke dough with yeast and flour
  2. Prepare the Toppings
  3. Flatten the dough ball and make it round
  4. Brush the dough with olive oil
  5. Spread the tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  6. Slide pizza into the oven
  7. Bake the pizza until the crust is browned and cheese is golden
